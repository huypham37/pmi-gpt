# Testing for Sensitive Information Sent via Unencrypted Channels

---
id: WSTG-CRYP-03
tag: TA

## Brief Summary

Sensitive data must be protected when it is transmitted through the network. If data is transmitted over HTTPS or encrypted in another way the protection mechanism must not have limitations or vulnerabilities, as explained in the broader article [Testing for Weak Transport Layer Security](01-Testing_for_Weak_Transport_Layer_Security.
